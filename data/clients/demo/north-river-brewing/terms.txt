North River Brewing Co. is a Quebec-based craft brewery that manufactures, cans, labels, markets, and sells beer and ready-to-drink alcoholic beverages across Canada.

The company sells beverages through retail partners, direct online orders where permitted, seasonal festival booths, and provincial distributors. Its products generally contain between 4% and 7% alcohol by volume.

Supplier and packaging terms require North River to comply with applicable food, beverage, packaging, and consumer protection laws. Product labels are reviewed annually unless a regulator requires faster updates.

Operational facts:
- Manufactures alcoholic beverages.
- Controls product packaging and labelling.
- Sells packaged alcoholic beverages to Canadian consumers.
- Uses third-party printers for can and bottle labels.
- Maintains a six-month packaging inventory cycle.
