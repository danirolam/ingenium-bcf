MapleCellars Marketplace operates a Canadian online marketplace for wines, spirits, and specialty alcoholic beverages sold by licensed suppliers.

MapleCellars does not manufacture beverages. It hosts supplier listings, processes customer orders, coordinates delivery with licensed partners, and requires suppliers to represent that listed products comply with applicable law.

Current supplier terms say: "Supplier is solely responsible for ensuring that all products, product descriptions, labels, warnings, packaging, and sales practices comply with applicable federal, provincial, and municipal laws."

Operational facts:
- Lists alcoholic beverages from third-party suppliers.
- Relies on supplier compliance warranties.
- Displays product images and label information online.
- May remove listings that create legal or reputational risk.
- Does not currently require a specific alcohol warning-label certification.
