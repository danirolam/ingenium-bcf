EventPour Technologies provides SaaS tools for festivals, venues, and event organizers that sell food and alcoholic beverages at events.

EventPour does not sell alcohol directly. The platform manages vendor onboarding, age-gate workflows, booth inventory, point-of-sale integrations, and vendor compliance checklists.

Customer terms say: "Customer and its vendors are responsible for obtaining all permits and complying with laws applicable to the sale, service, marketing, labelling, and distribution of regulated products."

Operational facts:
- Provides software infrastructure for alcohol sales at events.
- Does not own, package, or label beverages.
- Hosts vendor compliance workflows.
- Could update templates, checklists, and indemnity language.
- Risk is mostly indirect and contractual.
